*Originally written in Chinese, on Oct 29, 2025*

蓝色溢出了西部的天 <br>
白云惬意涂抹 <br>
是亲切的傍晚<br>

我听到了她的召唤<br>
我走上树梢<br>
我走上房顶<br>
端庄白色大理石的房顶<br>
为他人而建<br>
这时也为我加冕<br>

我走上了天空<br>
闭上眼笑<br>
这是无罪者的拥抱<br>
亦是怜悯<br>

地上人流的幻象<br>
惊恐着喊着<br>
“下来吧！你！”<br>
但走向各自的家<br>

我哼着歌<br>
童年和忧郁的歌<br>
向着追不上的鸟走去<br>
走进这无限的夜<br>

*English translation*

Blue spills across the western sky<br>
White clouds brush it at ease<br>
an intimate evening<br>

I hear her calling<br>
I step onto the treetops<br>
I step onto the rooftops<br>
The dignified white marble roofs<br>
built for others<br>
Now also crowning me<br>

I step into the sky<br>
Close my eyes and smile.<br>
This is the embrace of <br>
the innocent<br>
and also of mercy<br>

Below, the illusion of the crowd<br>
crying out in fear<br>
“Come down! You!”<br>
and walking on<br>
toward their own homes<br>

I hum a song<br>
a song of childhood and melancholy<br>
Walking toward a bird I cannot catch<br>
Walking<br>
into this endless night<br>
